APC 524 Final Project

Monitoring and predicting the 2024 U.S. elections

In light of the upcoming U.S. national elections, this project aims to monitor the pulse of voters across the country, predict the winners of the presidential and vice presidential race, and publish an interactive map that will be publicly accessible.

Leading up to Election Day, the project will regularly fetch poll/voting preference data which will feed into a prediction model to forecast the outcome of the elections. Post-elections, the prediction model will be validated by comparison with the final election result.

This project will be limited to monitoring the presidential and vice presidential race between the two major parties.
